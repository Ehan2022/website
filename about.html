about Us

Established in 2014 by Mr Mohammed Bin Quasem, Incredible Fashions Ltd. is an apparel manufacturer dedicated to creating employment opportunities in a safe and healthy working environment

The factory specializes in high-fashion knitted tops and offers a wide range of fabrics. The fabric base includes 100% Cotton, Viscose, Viscose/Lycra, Modal, and Bamboo with weights ranging from 90 to 250 gsm. We also offer garments which are manufactured from organically grown cotton, recycled cotton and recycled polyester in line with our sustainability vision.

IFL’s main asset is our skilled workforce, without whom the IFL family would not be where it is today within such a relatively short time. We take the utmost care to ensure that all workers are well compensated and are provided with a healthy environment where they can thrive - both personally and professionally.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to create high-quality knitwear products for export to global retailers while creating employment for Bangladeshi workers.

We aim to create and develop long-lasting relationships with customers who share our values and philosophy. We are dedicated to improving the lives of our workers by creating social and economic empowerment, all the while working to find solutions for environmental sustainability.

Working in an industry which provides access to employment to so many women is something we take great pride in. We believe that women’s socioeconomic empowerment greatly impacts the strength of households and communities. Our upper management consists of two women, who work tirelessly to ensure that the IFL women are lifted.
